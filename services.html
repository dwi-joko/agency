Audio / Video

The One Up Group is a leading supplier of high definition Audio / Video equipment for events of all sizes.

Our inventory of video projectors, professional grade
audio gear and concert quality audio systems gives us
an edge over the rest.

Our professional & technical background allow us to bridge these two elements to execute a seamless production. Whether you?re planning a small intimate conference meeting or a large-scale event, our production team has the capability to design and deliver audio and visual communication tools that address the need for more effective communication.

No mater the nature or scope of your event, rest assured The One Up Group can facilitate all of your Audio / Video needs.

From projectors & screens, LED Video Walls, projection mapping and more, to our state of the art microphone amplification systems, your messaging will be seen and heard loud and clear.
